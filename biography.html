Amigo dog training Biography

Dog training a labour of love:

I have been brought up as a Gaucho in a rural environment, surrounded by horses, cows, sheep and dogs. I was taught to understand animal behaviours and persuaded them to collaborate with human activities. I have been brought up with horses during the first years of my childhood, My horse was my best friend. Some years later my father bought me a Dobermann pincher with an impressive pedigree, She was trained for completion. I witnessed all training sessions and I knew that I wanted to be a Dog trainer at my early age of 8 years old. My Dobermann won some competitions.

K9 training and own Vet business

During my early 17 years old I started to work apprentice for a local Dog trainer, I learnt a lot about dog behaviours and training techniques. Then I was introduced to another Dog trainer. He hired my for my passion for this career. I worked with him for several years until I realised that I wanted to study Vet at university. I studied veterinary for some years at the university of Buenos Aires Argentina. Nonetheless my heart was always with dog’s behaviour. I quit university and returned to work as Dog trainer. Unfortunately my family did not support that decision. Later on my career I started to work with the German’s shepherd club where I learnt a lot of Dog exhibitions, training competitions and breading. I specialised on the K9 training.

I started my own business breeding black Belgium shepherd. I trained them and sold them as working dogs. I also carried on working as dog trainer and breeder for 15 years. Until I got a licence to Open a Vet shop in Buenos Aires. I worked as Director of my own Vet shop in Buenos Aires. I hired 3 vets. I am registered as Vet shop director in the Buenos Aires Vet college.

Moving to UK

Due to unprecedented circumstances I moved to the UK and started to work as a Dog trainer using K9 dog training. My trained dogs participated in two dog exhibitions in he UK, wining the first and second place. Currently I continues working as dog trainer helping families and dogs to be happier.
